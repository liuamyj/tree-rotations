Binary Tree Triangulations
This tool visualizes binary trees as polygon triangulations as described in the paper Rotation Distance, Triangulations, and Hyperbolic Geometry by Sleator, Tarjan, and Thurston.

To use: input a series of nodes in the order they should be inserted, separated by spaces. For example, to see a complete binary tree, try "4 2 6 1 3 5 7".
